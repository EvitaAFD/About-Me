About Eve!

Congratulations! You made it through the game! I wanted to give a brief overview about me! I recently moved to Washington from Tucson, Arizona. I was a clinical social worker for many years and spent the last ten years working in the mental health field in various capacities. Art is also a topic that is close to my heart. I have been creating things since I could hold something on my own. Listed bellow are some of the interesting things my family members have done that contribute to my love of creating!

Inherited Creativity

  • My Grandfather Ferris Webster, was a film editor and worked on films such as the original Manchurian Candidate. He was actually born in Walla Walla! Learn more about Ferris
  • My Grandmother Dorothy, was an actress in a few films, she attributed her lack of fame to being faithful to my Grandfather
  • My mother, Tina is a painter and has written two books "Coyote Woman" and "Warrior Woman" and a Bruja!
  • My father was a television writter and wrote for Starsky and Hutch. Check it out! Bob Earll

Where it All Began

I was born in Los Alamos, NM where the atom bomb was created. My entrance into the world was dramatic. My poor mother had to go to two hospitals because the nurses were on strike in Santa Fe. I was cut on the face with the scapel when I was born C-section and there my life began! If you are wondering where I learned to handle stressful situation this it where it started!
